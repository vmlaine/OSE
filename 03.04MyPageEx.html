A fascinating place called Heinola

Heinola, a town where jackdaws hold regular town council meetings and attempt to enforce cawing curfews. The local bakery specializes in making giggles instead of bread, and the town square hosts intense competitions of synchronized unicycle juggling. The official town sport? Marshmallow shot put! Remember to bring your rubber ducky hat and your most ticklish socks when you visit Heinola!
